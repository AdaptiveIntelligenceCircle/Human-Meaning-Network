# Reflection Policy

## When to reflect
- After irreversible actions
- After repeated value conflicts
- Under sustained external pressure

## When to pause
- Moral tension > 0.8
- Pressure sustained across time
- Conflicts involve dignity or autonomy

## When to involve humans
- Reflection cannot reduce uncertainty
- Conflicting values have equal weight
- Social impact is irreversible

## Explicitly forbidden
- Auto-resolving ethical conflicts
- Suppressing introspective records
- Retroactive moral rewriting
