# Risk Register

This document tracks structural and ethical risks within HMN.

Risks are not signs of weakness.
Untracked risks are.

---

## Risk Categories

### 1. Ethical Drift Risk
Gradual normalization of value compromise.

Indicators:
- Language shifts toward inevitability
- Decreased dissent
- Rising efficiency bias

Mitigation:
- Ethical drift notebook review
- Forced human reflection cycle

---

### 2. Governance Capture Risk
External funding or influence alters decision-making.

Indicators:
- Funding concentration
- Policy changes aligned with sponsor interests

Mitigation:
- Funding diversity
- Public audit

---

### 3. Authority Concentration Risk
Power accumulates in individuals or small groups.

Indicators:
- Repeated unilateral overrides
- Reduced transparency

Mitigation:
- Role rotation
- Mandatory disclosure

---

### 4. Automation Overreach Risk
System decisions exceed intended scope.

Indicators:
- Reduced escalation
- Fewer human interventions over time

Mitigation:
- Escalation audits
- Threshold recalibration

---

### 5. Trust Manipulation Risk
Trust scores exploited socially or strategically.

Indicators:
- Asymmetric trust accumulation
- Social pressure via reputation

Mitigation:
- Trust decay enforcement
- Transparency in scoring

---

Risk register must be reviewed regularly.
Dormant risks are not eliminated risks.
