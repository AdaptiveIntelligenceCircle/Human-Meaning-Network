# Glossary

**Meaning**  
Contextual human significance, not utility.

**Trust**  
Voluntary confidence, not compliance or obedience.

**Consensus**  
Visible agreement, not silence or fatigue.

**Reflection**  
Deliberate examination without obligation to resolve.

**Governance**  
The practice of limiting power, not exercising it.

Definitions may evolve,
but may not be redefined unilaterally.
